MacroTracker by EPSAIT is a calorie, macro, meal, and activity tracker with free core tracking and optional Premium AI features.

Log meals manually, search food databases, scan packaged products, save frequent meals, create recipes, and review your daily calories, protein, carbs, fats, water, and activity from one focused app.

Key features:

- Daily calorie and macro tracking
- Food diary with meal history
- Recipe library and quick meal logging
- Barcode scanning for packaged foods
- Optional AI-assisted meal drafts from text or photos
- Weekly nutrition insights and goal adjustments
- Optional Health Connect import for sleep, workouts, steps, distance, and calories burned
- Optional meal reminders
- Optional encrypted Google Drive backup
- Home screen summary widget
- Light and dark themes
- English and Spanish support

Privacy:

MacroTracker is local-first. Your meal diary, recipes, settings, body progress, and activity data are stored on your device by default. Optional features may send limited data outside the device only when you choose to use them: food search, AI meal interpretation, anonymous crash diagnostics, and Google Drive backup. Health Connect data is read only after Android permission approval and is used to show activity and wellness context inside the app.

MacroTracker is not a medical device. Nutrition, activity, and AI-generated data may be incomplete or inaccurate. Review important values yourself and consult a qualified professional for medical, pregnancy, illness, or clinical nutrition decisions.
