MacroTracker by EPSAIT is an AI-powered calorie counter, macro tracker, and nutrition diary that helps you log meals in seconds — from text descriptions, photos, or manual entry.

Whether you are counting macros for muscle gain, tracking calories for weight loss, or following a custom diet plan from your nutritionist, MacroTracker keeps your food diary organized and your progress visible.

Why MacroTracker?

🤖 AI Meal Logging
Describe your meal in plain text or snap a photo. The AI calorie counter creates an editable macro draft — review protein, carbs, fats, and calories before saving. No more guessing portion sizes.

📊 Complete Macro Tracker
Track daily calories, protein, carbohydrates, fats, fiber, sugar, and water intake. See your nutrition breakdown at a glance with weekly insights and goal adjustments.

🔍 Food Database & Barcode Scanner
Search food databases or scan packaged product barcodes for instant nutritional data. Save frequent meals and build a personal recipe library for one-tap logging.

👩‍⚕️ Built for Nutritionists
Nutritionists and dietitians can create personalized macro plans for their clients. Clients share aggregate progress snapshots — not raw diary data — keeping coaching efficient and private.

💪 Activity & Health Tracking
Import sleep, workouts, steps, distance, and calories burned from Health Connect or Apple Health. Track body weight progress and daily wellness habits.

🔒 Privacy-First Design
Your meal diary, recipes, and body data stay on your device by default. Optional encrypted Google Drive backup. Optional anonymous crash reporting. No ads, no selling your data.
